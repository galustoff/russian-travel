Here, in the midst of forests and sand dunes, you can see two water horizons - a calm Curonian Lagoon from one side and rippled by the waves of the Baltic Sea with another. A unique natural area on the edge of the Russian enclave.

This is not the end of the Kaliningrad region. Altai

URL

Facebook

Altai is one of the most beautiful places in Russia. Primarily because of the mountains: if you go along the ridge, you will see slopes covered with pine trees, mountain rivers and lakes. And if you open windows in the car, you can get to know with the invisible miracle of these places - mountain air.

The climate in Altai is moderate, so it is best to go here in summer. So you will see all the variety local flora and fauna. Elk roam the forests of Altai, eagles fly over the ridges, and roe deer graze on the plains. And the famous Pallas' cat are also inhabitants of the Altai Territory.

Karelia

URL

http://vodlozero.ru/

Siberia ends not in the Urals, but in Karelia: the Siberian larch that forms the taiga does not grow west of Vodlozero. But here it stretches 30 meters - the forests of the Karelian national parks because of the impenetrable swamps, they never knew the ax. Some pines are already more than half a millennium. Touch a living creature that has seen the sun before Ivan saw it Grozny. In the virgin forest for a hundred kilometers you will not find a trail. And on rare paths trees a couple of meters from the ground are marked with bear claws. So that everyone knows who is the boss here.
